Eligibility and Application Process

Please find below details step to be followed as part of the admission process

  • Complete Application

    Fill program application form and pay one time registration fees of Rs. 1500

  • Selection Test

    Appear for a 30 min online Aptitude test, consisting of 15 questions encompasing Quantitative Aptitude, Verbal reasoning logical reasoning and Data interpretation

  • Get shortlisted and receive offer

    The candidates will be evaluated based on their profile i.e., professional experience, graduation and post graduation degree scores, and selection test. Select candidates may be asked to appear for an online interview

  • Block Your Seat

    Upon receiving the offer letter from NMIMS Global Access, block seat by paying a seat reservation amount of Rs. 40000

  • Document submission

    Submit self attested documents

  • Balance Closure

    Pay the balance fee. 0% interest EMI options available
